Privacy Policy

Habiros does not collect, store, or share personal user data with third parties.

Local Data Storage

All data created within the app, including habits, progress, levels, and statistics, is stored locally on the user’s device.

Data Export and Import

Habiros allows users to export and import their data in JSON format. This data is fully controlled by the user and is not transmitted to external servers.

No Personal Data Collection

The app does not require user registration and does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or location.

Notifications

Habiros may use local notifications to remind users about their habits. These notifications are optional and can be enabled or disabled at any time.

No Tracking

Habiros does not use analytics services, tracking tools, or third-party data collection services.

Changes to This Policy

If future updates introduce features that involve data collection, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
