Privacy Policy

Habiros does not collect, store, or share personal user data with third parties.

All data created within the app, including habits, progress, levels, and statistics, is stored locally on the user’s device.

Habiros provides an optional feature that allows users to export and import their data in JSON format. This data is fully controlled by the user and is not transmitted to any external servers.

The app does not require user registration and does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or location.

Habiros may use local notifications to remind users about their habits. These notifications are optional and can be enabled or disabled at any time.

Habiros does not use analytics services or third-party tracking tools.

If future updates introduce features that involve data collection, this policy will be updated.
